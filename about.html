About Me

Relay Protection & Testing Engineer · POWERGRID · 400/220 kV Substation

I'm Pratap Kumar, an electrical engineer working at a 400/220 kV POWERGRID substation. My work is in relay protection and testing, with hands-on experience across:

  • AIS — Air Insulated Switchgear
